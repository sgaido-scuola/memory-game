The rules

How to play

1. You play in turns. Player 1 starts.

2. On your turn, flip two cards: first an orange one (definition), then a blue one (term).

3. When you flip a card, read it aloud to your partner!

4. If the cards match, you score a point and play again.

5. If they don't match, the cards flip back and it's the other player's turn.

6. Two rounds, six pairs each. The player with more total pairs wins!
